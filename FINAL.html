Who Are We?

The Young Innovators Club (YIC) of Xavier University Junior High School, under the guidance of Club Moderator Ms. Cherry Ann L. Mahunyag, is a community of young scientists, researchers, and innovators. We believe science extends beyond the classroom and comes alive through innovation, environmental stewardship, technology, and research. Through hands-on learning and real-world problem-solving, we empower students to become responsible leaders and future innovators who create positive change in the community.

Whether you aspire to develop sustainable solutions, design technological innovations, or conduct your first research project, YIC is where curiosity becomes creation and ideas become impact.

YIC Achievement & Milestone

Since its establishment, the Young Innovators Club has fostered a community of students passionate about Science, Technology, and Environment. Through hands-on investigations, innovation challenges, sustainability initiatives, competitions, educational trips, and community-centered projects, the club has empowered students to develop creative solutions, promote environmental responsibility, and make meaningful contributions to the school and wider community.
